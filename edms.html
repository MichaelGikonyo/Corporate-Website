About EDMS

CCoseke has provided and continues to provide EDMS solutions to our clients in diverse industries, both in the public and in the private sector.
These come coupled with workflows that can easily be integrated with existing solutions in use by client organizations. With Coseke your organization will experience seamless use of organization’s software solutions.

Organizations produce piles of paper documents, images and other information electronically. Vital documents like contracts, proposals, agreements, letters, technical renderings are crucial for success yet many organizations have them stored all over the place – on desktop computers, network drives, email, USB sticks and in various consumer file sharing sites.

Managing your information electronically has many benefits, Coseke helps you get started and make the transition successful.
